> h 标签

h1、h2、h3 顺序排列，不能跳过，不能乱序，不要只是为了让字体变大而使用 h 标签，H 标签代表是页面标题。

h1 freeCodeCamp

h2 Learn Front-End Development

h2 Learn Back-End Development

> i 标签

idiomatic text element，本来是为了让文字变成斜体，现在是为了 highlighting alternative voice or mood, idiomatic terms from another language, technical terms, and thoughts.

不是为了突出文字的重要性，只是表示这个文字与周边的文字不同。

There is a certain je ne sais quoi in the air.

> em 标签

em 标签用来强调东西，类似一句话重音的地方

Never give up on your dreams.

如果只是要斜体效果，而没别的意思要表达，应该使用 css 实现

> b 标签为了高亮关键词或者产品名称，吸引读者的注意

We tested several products, including the SuperSound 3000 for audio quality, the QuickCharge Pro for fast charging, and the EcoClean Vacuum for cleaning. The first two performed well, but the EcoClean Vacuum did not meet expectations.

> strong 标签

为了强调重要性，紧急性

Warning: This product may cause allergic reactions.
